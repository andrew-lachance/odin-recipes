White Russian


Description

A cocktail made from vodka, heavy cream, and coffee liqueur.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 1 ounce coffee liqueur
  • 1 ounce heavy cream
  • 4 cubes clear ice

Steps

  1. Add vodka and coffee liqueur to a glass.
  2. Add ice and heavy cream.
  3. Stir gently and serve.
